# auditorial-a-plus-notes
Documenting my CompTIA A+ Journey through study notes, cheat sheets, flashcards, and simplified explanations designed to help reinforce foundational IT concepts for myself and others on a similar path. Focused on making technical topics more approachable through practical examples and organized resources.
I currently use CompTIA CertMaster primarily to test and reinforce my CompTIA A+ knowledge. For studying and learning the material itself, I combine several resources, including Professor Messer’s YouTube videos and CompTIA A+ notes, the TIA Education Udemy course by Andrew Ramdayal (including the notes and resources provided throughout the course), and the CompTIA A+ Complete Review Guide by Troy McMillan.

To better understand concepts, I also use Microsoft Copilot AI as a supplemental learning tool by asking questions, requesting clarifications, and organizing information based on my current understanding of the material.

The materials shared in this repository are personalized study resources created throughout my learning journey. Any simplified explanations, summaries, interpretations, or flashcards are intended solely for educational and personal study. Official CompTIA terminology and definitions will remain distinguished where applicable.
